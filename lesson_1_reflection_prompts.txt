How did viewing a diff between two versions of a file help you see the bug that
was introduced?

It shows what lines were edited, either removed or added, which makes finding the bug easier because you aren’t wasting your time reading lines of code that haven’t be modified and therefore aren’t going to be the cause of the bug.
